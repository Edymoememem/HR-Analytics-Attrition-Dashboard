# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard — Attrition Analysis | Syntecxhub Internship

A single-page interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze employee attrition patterns across demographics, departments, salary slabs, job roles, and business travel — enabling HR teams to identify high-risk groups and make data-driven retention decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of my ongoing internship at **Syntecxhub**. The goal was to analyze HR data for 1,480 employees and uncover the key drivers of attrition — helping organizations understand *who* is leaving, *when*, and *why*.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Age group 26–35** has the highest attrition count — early career employees are the most at-risk group
- **Employees earning under $5K** account for the majority of attrition — salary is a primary driver
- **Research & Development** department leads with 133 attritions, followed by Sales at 93
- **Year 1 of employment** sees a dramatic attrition spike of 50 employees — onboarding and early engagement are critical
- **Single employees** are significantly more likely to leave (25.37%) compared to married (12.37%)
- **Technical Degree holders** have the highest attrition rate by education field (25.93%)
